My First Blog About My Favorite Art

October 5, 2024 by Justine

My favorite film is waves by Trey Edward-Schultz. He is is a wicked man for creating that. I almost hate him for it. It's the best thing I have ever seen and I wish that I could re-watch it for the first time again.

It had such an insane impact on me. I yearn for that feeling. The only thing that has made me feel something similar is NEON GENESIS EVANGELION. And that was insane huh? The real ones get it. I think about this film at least once every month. And I feel so sad and sentimental thinking about how the story went. If I could, I would live in that film. The second act only... The first act was torture. I will make a film that impacts people like Trey.

My 2nd Blog About My Favorite Artist

October 6, 2024 by Justine

My favorite artist is FRANK OCEAN. I love him so much it's ridiculous. His art speaks to me in a way that nobody has in my life before.

I love evry piece of music he has released. Just kidding! There are obviously some songs that I've listened to so much that i kind of hate now. But the fact still remains. He means so much to me. I wish I could meet him in New York, and I will. This is just filler so that the text can wrap around the image. But I will say I hope Frank is doing well. He has helped me through some of the toughest times in my life though. I sound insane but, alas. Oh! And he is gorgeous.
